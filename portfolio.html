Monster in the Mirror

2024

My short film "Monster in the Mirror" explores the themes of femininity and Eurocentric beauty, and the toll these subjects have on young minority girls who are blossoming into their adolescence. Engaging with critical text and poetry that explore the themes of ethnicity and feeling sub-human, "Monster in the Mirror" aims to explore these feelings in the format of a coming-of-age story.
